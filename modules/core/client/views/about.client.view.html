ABOUT US

BooKKing mission is to minimise the wastage of books and thereby reduce the wastage of paper which comes at the cost of cutting down of trees. Everyone uses a book but not everyone take steps to plant trees. So if we may not contribute in growing trees we must contribute in reducing the number of trees cutting down every year. And that is by the reuse of books. We provide efficient ways to reuse the books by enabling users to sell their books to any other user. Also we provide book bank structure for schools to enable administration of the books for Book Bank Management.
