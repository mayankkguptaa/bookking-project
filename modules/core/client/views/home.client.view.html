Book Bank Administration

Book bank have the facility for all students to get books within an educational unit. The Books from the Book Bank are issued for the whole year or each semester. Student members can issue books for whole year or semester. And after the books are returned back to the system books are refurbished and then recirculated to the new batch. It has many benefits over the current system as it reduced the cost of having the books for education and also it saves the wastage of books i.e papers which are made from cutting down of trees.
